ABOUT US

In 2018, Jayson Ferrer and Elaine Ferrer launched the company as an online business. Towards the end of that year, they began to expand their team, and by 2019, they had acquired nine dynamic individuals, some of whom were eventually promoted. Despite the pandemic that hit the world and the economy in 2020, the business continued to thrive and even introduced a promising product called MagneZIum, which helped many people during the pandemic.

In 2021, the company has continued to expand its reach by venturing into outbound sales and acquiring more salespeople. Additionally, they are investing in the training of their team members and planning to expand their product offerings. The company has plans to build its own office building and warehouse by 2023. Despite having been in operation for four years, the company remains committed to improving and innovating. They are still in the process of identifying individuals who share their mission and vision.
